    Detailed Description

    Avoidance module.

    Avoidance is split in 2 steps:

    1. avoidance_build_graph(): build graph of avoidance path to reach finish pose, including obstacles avoidance.
    2. avoidance_get_pose(): get intermediate pose in avoidance computed path.

    Direct segment to reach final position can be checked to see if it becomes free of obstacles with avoidance_check_recompute()
